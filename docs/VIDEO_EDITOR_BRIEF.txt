1. WHAT IS OPENCLAW?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

OpenClaw is an open-source AI agent. Think of it like a personal assistant that
actually DOES things — not just chats.

Most AI chatbots only answer questions. OpenClaw can:
  • Run tasks on your computer
  • Browse the web, fill forms, research topics
  • Connect to your messaging apps (Telegram, Discord, Slack, WhatsApp)
  • Remember things and work on schedules
  • Use files, code, and tools — like a real assistant with access to your stuff

It connects to AI models (Claude, GPT, etc.) and your real tools and data.
Like having a smart coworker that never sleeps.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. WHAT PROBLEMS DOES IT SOLVE?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

PROBLEM 1: AI that only talks, doesn't act
  • Regular chatbots just respond. OpenClaw executes. You say "book that flight"
    and it can actually go do it.

PROBLEM 2: Setting it up is hard
  • OpenClaw is powerful but technical. You need to install it, configure it,
    run servers. Most people don't have time for that.

PROBLEM 3: AI costs add up fast
  • Using the best AI for every little task is expensive. You want the right
    tool for the job — affordable for simple stuff, best when it matters.

PROBLEM 4: Your AI is stuck in one app
  • You want your agent on Telegram, in your browser, on Discord — everywhere.
    OpenClaw connects to all of them.

PROBLEM 5: Security and privacy
  • You want your own agent, your own data. Not shared with thousands of others.
